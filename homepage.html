This website was designed to help strengthen people's ability for arithmetic.

Welcome to Flash Math where you can train your math skills.

On the top right, if you hover your cursor over arithmetic, there will be a dropdown allowing you to choose from 4 different categories. Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, and Division. For each of these modes, you will be given one minute to try and solve 15 questions. If needed, a pencil and paper might come in handy but don't rely on it. Try not to use your calculator as this helps with your mental math abilities, not your calculator abilities. If these 4 modes are too easy for you, try out the challenge mode with 30 difficult questions but no timer. There will also be videos embedded next to the practices for help. Good Luck and Enjoy.

Although it may seem that mental math is a simple and basic concept that almost anyone can grasp, many overlook the fact that mental math truly is the foundation of advanced math to come. While the basics of mental math is usually taught around an elementary level, it is necessary to keep practicing mental math once in a while to keep the brain quick and sharp. For example, when test-taking, or even going out shopping, it is beneficial to have these quick skills to save time. The brain is a muscle. And, as most other muscles in the human body, the brain can get stronger with more exercise. In fact, mental math is so important, that we may not even realize that we are doing mental math in our every day-to-day lives. Helping kids with mental math at an early age can really boost their knowledge of math and efficient problem solving, which is crucial to the real world.
